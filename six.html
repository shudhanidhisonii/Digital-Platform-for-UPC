Loni Road Flyover and Widening Project

Project Type:

Flyover construction and road widening

Objective:

  • The flyover aims to ease traffic congestion on Loni Road, a highly congested route during peak hours.
  • It will help accommodate increasing traffic from nearby areas and improve connectivity.
  • Expected to provide a smoother passage for vehicles traveling between Ghaziabad and Delhi, reducing bottlenecks.

Location:

The flyover will be constructed along Loni Road, stretching through parts of Ghaziabad and connecting key NCR areas.

Timeline:

  • Start Date: 20 Oct 2020 (Part of Uttar Pradesh Government infrastructure plans).
  • Expected Completion: 2024-2025.

Scope:

  • It will cover critical points of Loni Road where traffic bottlenecks are prevalent.
  • Constructed with multiple lanes to ensure better flow and accommodate more vehicles.

Impact on Local Areas:

The flyover will improve connectivity between Ghaziabad and Delhi, reducing travel time and easing traffic flow.
